Experimental and Behavioral Economics

Experimental Economics

It offers, and continuously develops, a specific methodology
“economic experimentsФ

In principle, the experiments can be designed around classic models,
without any non-classic behavioral consideration

However, “behavioral deviationsФ will be inevitable observed
→ Behavioral Economics

Experimental and Behavioral Economics

Behavioral Economics

It studies and explores behavioral deviations from the standard economic theory

By doing so, it contributes to the development of:
(i) new models
(ii) modifications and upgrades of the existing theories

However, Лfood for thoughtС frequently comes from the experiments,
which are also necessary for testing (and fine-tuning) the models
→ Experimental Economics
