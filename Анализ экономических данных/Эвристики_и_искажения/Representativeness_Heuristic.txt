REPRESENTATIVENESS HEURISTIC
Kursov Barsukov Vodolatskyy

WHAT IS IT?
DEFINITION
Mental shortcut for judging the probability of an outcome in terms of how well it seems to represent or match a particular prototype

Questions:
• What is the probability that object A belongs to class B?
• What is the probability that event A originates from process B?
• What is the probability that process B will generate event A?

WHY GAMBLERS LOSE?
Let’s play a game

• The gambler's fallacy, also known as the Monte Carlo fallacy, occurs when an individual erroneously believes that a certain random event is less likely or more likely to happen based on the outcome of a previous event or series of events.
• This can also be implied towards the stock market when you are an aggressive trader.

HOW RACISM WORKS?

In theory:
• Racism is based on Pygmalion Effect.
• The phenomenon of a person’s subconscious misconception of judging strangers founded on their appearance and person’s social experience.

In practice:
• Overall, black Americans are arrested at 2.6 times the per-capita rate of all other Americans.
• This ratio is higher for:
  - Murder: 6.3 times
  - Robbery: 8.1 times
